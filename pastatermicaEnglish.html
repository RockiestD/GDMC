Thermal paste and spatula

Thermal paste is used to improve heat transfer between the processor (CPU) and the cooler, avoiding overheating and ensuring better performance. The spatula helps to spread the paste evenly. evenly over the CPU, avoiding air bubbles and ensuring complete coverage.

Basic steps:

1 - Clean the surface of the CPU and cooler.

2 - Apply a small amount of paste to the center of the CPU.

3 - Use the spatula to spread the paste in a thin, uniform layer.

4 - Install the cooler, ensuring good pressure for efficient thermal transfer.

These items are essential for effective cooling and durability of the computer.
