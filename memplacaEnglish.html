Placing the RAM memories on the motherboard

To install RAM memories on the motherboard, follow these steps:

Prepare the environment: Make sure you are in a clean area and wear an anti-static wrist strap to prevent component damage.

Align the RAM modules with the slots, watching the key to ensure they are inserted correctly.

Insert the RAM modules into the slots with even pressure until you hear a "click", indicating that they have locked into place.

Check that the RAM is securely seated and go to the next step.
