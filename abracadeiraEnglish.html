Nylon Cable Ties

Nylon cable ties are essential for organizing cables when assembling computers. They improve air flow inside the case, preventing components from heating, in addition to leaving the assembly with a professional and safe appearance, preventing cables from coming loose or touching in sensitive areas.

Main uses:

Cable Grouping: Facilitates organization and access during maintenance. Fixing: Keeps cables firmly on the sides of the cabinet, freeing up central space. Aesthetics: Provides a clean, organized appearance, ideal for cabinets with windows.

For efficient use, it is recommended to choose different sizes of cable ties, avoid tightening excess and cut the ends for a better finish. Nylon cable ties are a practical solution for organization and security inside the computer.
