Computer desk

The case is essential for the computer, as it protects and organizes components such as the motherboard, processor and disks. It prevents physical damage, dust and keeps the system running safely.

In addition to protection, the cabinet is fundamental in thermal control, with openings and support for fans that ensure air circulation. This prevents overheating and extends the life of the internal components.

It also makes installation and maintenance easier, offering space for upgrades and cable management. Well-designed models make the system more efficient and simplify future adjustments.

Finally, the case complements the computer's design, ranging from simple models to optional options. customizable. Thus, it combines protection, performance and style in a single component.
